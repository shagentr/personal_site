About Me

Hello, world!

I am an experienced Business Intelligence / Data Analyst of approximately seven years. After graduating from Arizona State University in 2019 I accepted my first role in Data Ops - a Data Analyst position at ZipRecruiter that I would never have applied for had it not been for the strong urging of my then colleagues (to whom I am forever grateful), and one that would kick off a life-long passion for everything data.

If you're here to learn more about or connect with me regarding my professional life please see my resume or socials and don't hesitate to connect with me on LinkedIn or email me!

Life isn't all about work, though! If you'd simply like to learn a little about me personally please click here where I share a bit about my life outside the office and the things I enjoy.
